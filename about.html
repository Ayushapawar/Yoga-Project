Our Journey to Wellness

Our Story

Aroma Yoga Centre is more than just a yoga studio; it is a community dedicated to the holistic development of the mind, body, and soul. We believe that yoga is a way of life that extends beyond the mat.

We are committed to traditional yoga practices that have been passed down through generations, ensuring that our students receive the most authentic experience possible.

Meet Your Trainer

Priyanka Raut

A passionate and dedicated yoga trainer, Priyanka Raut leads Aroma Yoga Centre with a vision to bring authentic traditional yoga to everyone. Her teaching focuses on correct postures, breathing techniques (Pranayama), and mindfulness.

Under her guidance, students learn to achieve a perfect balance between physical fitness and mental tranquility.
